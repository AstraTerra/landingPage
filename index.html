How the magic happens

Tldr we are a platform that makes it accessible for anyone to short or long overall markets and risks

AstraTerra is a platform that makes it possible to tokenize nearly any asset, risk,market or event; a concept never seen before. Our system of vaults makes it possible to leverage or short any token on our platform, Making it suitable for investors of all stripes and risk tolerances. All assets based on real world numbers are at least 200% colloratized, protecting their peg. Assets based on probability will be determined by market forces with our vaults enabling short/long positions.

Governance

Governance is done through the HMKT token which enables users to vote for a board of directors. This board will be made up of 7 members, each serving a one year term. If any directors act in a manner that is not in the best interest of AstraTerra, they are recalled and new elections take place. The reason we have chosen this governance model instead of the more popular direct democracy is that a small group of representatives can make decisions much quicker, as well as there being less room for populism to derail the project. Any change to this system will require a 75% supermajority to be approved.
